Training Routine:

Warmup:

  • 250 aimbots kill ~ 180 Flicks / Mini Sprays and keep moving no standing still (still counter strafe properly just don't stand still for long), flick aggressively (10 mins)
  • Fast aim/reflex training ~ (15 minutes) Try spray for the head constantly and transfer between body
  • FFA DM 25-35 mins ~ Focus on spraying for the head.

Total Minutes:
Max: 60 - Min: 45

Full-Routine

  • Warm-up No shooting mouse movement to heads (5 mins) Aimbotz
  • 180 Flicks / Mini Sprays and keep moving no standing still (still counter strafe properly just don't stand still for long), flick aggressively (10 mins) Aimbotz
  • Run Stop Flick - Right Left & Mini Sprays (10 mins) Aimbotz
  • Spray Transfers - All guns that are needed(try not to go over the number of bullets you have in a normal mag) (10 mins) Aimbotz
  • (Optional) Combine all of the above / Awp Flicks (5 -10 mins) Aimbotz
  • Prefire maps(do only active duty maps) 2 at a time (15-25 mins)
  • KZ training (Focus on making correct air strafes and making each jump as smoothly as possible) (15 mins or more)
  • FFA DM 30-45 mins or 250 kills ~ Focus on spraying for the head.

Total Minutes:
Max: 130 - Min: 100
